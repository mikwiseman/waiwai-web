Finding Clients with WaiWai: A Step-by-Step Guide

Apr 10, 2025

WaiWai is a lead generation system for the B2B segment. We explain how WaiWai organizes the sales process - from finding and ranking clients to writing emails and systematizing results.

1. Finding Clients

  • First, we work together with the client to create a clear image of the potential customer. Instead of dry technical specifications, we simply have a conversation with you to find out together who could become your ideal client. This live dialogue allows for more precise determination of what leads we're looking for. In parallel, we integrate WaiWai's analytical toolkit for target audience modeling.
    Key profile parameters include: position, location, industry, company size. These can also include hobbies, language skills, education, marital status, social media activity, job searching. This forms what we call the Ideal Customer Profile at WaiWai.

  • Next, WaiWai begins searching in numerous business information databases. For example, these could be Apollo, ZoomInfo, or Wiza.
    We obtain some information ourselves through parsing from open sources. We also additionally enrich data, for example, from social networks.

2. Evaluating the Compiled Client List

Having formed the final lead list, WaiWai ranks the database according to Ideal Customer Profile criteria. We write first to the most likely leads.

We integrate with any possible CRMs: Amo CRM, Hubspot. WaiWai also integrates with Google Docs or Yandex Docs.

3. Writing Emails to Clients

Guided by the data set about each person individually, WaiWai sends personalized emails.

4. Assisting the Sales Department

WaiWai does everything to make the sales manager's job easier. The system can:

  • Compile questions for conducting calls

  • Analyze managers' calls, for example, how well they followed the script.

  • Sometimes the sales department is overloaded with incoming customer requests and needs help - WaiWai sorts them and ranks by urgency. The system responds to clients automatically and can perform specified actions, for example - transferring requests to a partner network.

  • Outbound assistance - if a sales manager wants to write to a specific company, WaiWai will find potentially interested people there. The system will collect information about potential clients and contact them.

Thus, WaiWai performs the work of an entire sales department - from targeted client search to assisting sales managers in closing deals.
